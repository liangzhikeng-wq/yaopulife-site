## How to Choose the Right Gift

Consider these factors:

- **Timeline:** Moon lamps and name signs ship fastest (5-7 days). Embroidery and needle felt need 10-18 days.
- **Budget:** Coasters ($28) and name signs ($35) are affordable. Needle felt portraits ($65-89) are premium.
- **Their style:** Minimalist couples → embroidery or name sign. Eclectic couples → needle felt or resin art. Romantic couples → moon lamp.
- **Occasion weight:** Low-key anniversary → coasters or text lamp. Major milestone → custom portrait or wedding embroidery.

## Make It Extra Special

A few tips to elevate any personalized gift:

1. **Include a handwritten note** explaining why you chose this specific piece
2. **Gift wrap matters** - kraft paper, dried flowers, wax seal = instant upgrade
3. **Time it right** - order 2-3 weeks early to avoid rush fees
4. **Pair with something small** - a custom gift + a bottle of their favorite wine = perfect
