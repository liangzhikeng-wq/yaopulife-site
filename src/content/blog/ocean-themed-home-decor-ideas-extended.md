## Introduction

Coastal farmhouse style blends the rustic warmth of farmhouse aesthetics with the fresh, airy feel of coastal design. It's about creating spaces that feel both inviting and relaxed — like a beach house that welcomes you home.

In this guide, we'll explore how to incorporate handmade pieces into your coastal farmhouse decor, creating a unique space that reflects your personal style.

## Section 1: Understanding Coastal Farmhouse Style

### The Core Elements

**Rustic Warmth + Airy Light**
Coastal farmhouse combines weathered wood textures, natural linens, and soft ocean colors. The result is a space that feels grounded yet breezy.

**Handmade Authenticity**
Handcrafted pieces add authenticity and character. Each handmade item carries the maker's care and attention, creating visual warmth that mass-produced items lack.

**Personal Touches**
Customized items — from family name signs to photo moon lamps — transform a house into a home that tells your family's story.

### Color Palettes for Coastal Farmhouse

- **Soft Blues**: Think morning sky over calm water
- **Warm Whites**: Beach sand, natural linens
- **Weathered Wood**: Driftwood tones, barn wood finishes
- **Greens**: Sea glass, eucalyptus, coastal plants
- **Warm Neutrals**: Sand, shell, sun-bleached tones
