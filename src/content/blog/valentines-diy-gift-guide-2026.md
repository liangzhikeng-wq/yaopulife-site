## How to Choose the Perfect DIY Gift

Selecting the ideal Valentine's gift requires considering several factors:

### Consider Their Interests
- Do they love pets? A custom pet portrait shows you understand their passion.
- Are they into home decor? A family name sign or moon lamp adds personal style to their space.
- Do they appreciate art? Needle-felted or embroidered pieces demonstrate craftsmanship they can display proudly.

### Think About Their Lifestyle
- Space constraints: Smaller items like embroidery hoops work well in apartments.
- Travel frequency: Portable items like custom accessories make great traveling companions.
- Home ownership: Larger pieces like wall signs suit homeowners.

### Set a Budget
DIY gifts range from $28 to $100+, allowing you to find something within your comfort zone while still providing exceptional value and personal touch.
